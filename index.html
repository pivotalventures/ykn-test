Services

Unlock your potential and soar to new heights with iFLiUSA! Our comprehensive training programs are designed to turn your aviation dreams into reality. Whether you're a beginner looking to take your first solo flight or an experienced pilot aiming for advanced certifications, we have the courses and expertise to help you succeed. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced instructors, and flexible pricing options, there's never been a better time to pursue your passion for flight. Contact us today to get started on your journey to becoming a skilled and confident pilot!

Private Pilot Training Package

Our private pilot training package includes a minimum of 40 flight hours and 20 hours of ground instruction. Work closely with our experienced instructors to master the skills needed to earn your private pilot's license and take to the skies with confidence.

Instrument Rating Course

Take your flying skills to the next level with our instrument rating course. This comprehensive program covers advanced navigation techniques and instrument flight rules (IFR), preparing you to fly in a wider range of weather conditions and airspace environments.

Commercial Pilot Program

Our commercial pilot program is designed for aspiring professional pilots. With a minimum of 250 flight hours and rigorous training in advanced maneuvers and commercial flight operations, you'll be well-prepared to pursue a career in aviation.

Aircraft Rental

Already a licensed pilot? Rent one of our well-maintained aircraft for your personal or business travel needs. Our competitive rental rates make it easy to take to the skies whenever you want.
